Spiral Coil Inductance $$L = \frac{(NR)^2}{8R + 11W}$$

  1. L = inductance of coil in microhenrys (µH)
  2. R = average radius of the coil in inches
  3. N = number of turns
  4. W = width of the coil in inches

Helical Coil Inductance $$L = \frac{(NR)^2}{9R + 10H}$$

  1. L = inductance of coil in microhenrys (µH)
  2. N = number of turns
  3. R = radius of coil in inches (Measure from the center of the coil to the middle of the wire.)
  4. H = height of coil in inches

Energy Storage Mechanisms $$J = \frac{V^2 C}{2}$$ $$J = \frac{I^2 L}{2}$$
